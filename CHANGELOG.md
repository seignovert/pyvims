[Release 1.1.0 - 2026-03-24][1.1.0]
-----------------------------------
[1.1.0]: https://github.com/seignovert/pyvims/releases/tag/1.1.0

### Added
- A CHANGELOG 🙌
- PyVIMS is now available on [conda-forge](https://anaconda.org/conda-forge/pyvims)

### Changed
- Remove `setup.py` and `setup.cfg` in favor of `pyproject.toml`.
- Remove `requirements.txt` in favor of `uv.lock`.
- Remove `flake8` and `pylint` linters in favor of `ruff`.


[Release 1.0.5 - 2024-10-22][1.0.5]
-----------------------------------
[1.0.5]: https://github.com/seignovert/pyvims/releases/tag/1.0.5

### Fixed
- Numpy depreciated `np.product` and `OverflowError` error on `uint8` mask.
